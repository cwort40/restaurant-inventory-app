About Inventory Tracker

Discover the power and ease of managing your inventory with this state-of-the-art application!

Inventory Tracker is a web application designed to help users keep track of their inventory and manage their menu items. The app is built with Django and makes use of several class-based views to provide a user-friendly interface for managing ingredients, purchases, and menu items.

With Inventory Tracker, users can easily view, add, update, and delete ingredients from their inventory. The app also provides a search function to quickly find specific ingredients. Additionally, users can keep track of purchases by adding, updating, and deleting items from the purchases list.

The app's menu management features allow users to create and manage their menu items, including their ingredients and recipe requirements. With this information, Inventory Tracker can also provide a convenient method of tracking the availability of menu items based on the current inventory levels of their ingredients.

Inventory Tracker is a secure platform that requires users to log in to access its features. Whether you're a small business owner, restaurant manager, or just someone who wants to keep track of their inventory, Inventory Tracker is the solution you've been looking for.
